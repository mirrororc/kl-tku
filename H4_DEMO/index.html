展出介紹

攝影藝術家在這個展覽帶來創造力、多樣性，以及視覺和思想上的衝擊。

透過鏡頭，這些才華橫溢的年輕藝術家捕捉了我們周圍世界的複雜、美麗和脆弱。

他們的作品挑戰我們以新的方式看待事物，探索人類經驗的眾多面向以及弔詭之處。

這些展示的影像試圖與觀眾在情感建立聯繫、形而上的思考。

在混亂和不確定性中，藝術可以提供一束指引的光芒。

我們期待與您分享這個經驗。

In this exhibition, the photography artists bring creativity, diversity, and either visually and philosophically impact. Through their lenses, these talented young artists capture the complexity, beauty, and fragility of the world around us. Their work challenges us to see things in new ways, to explore the many facets of the human experience, and to confront its paradoxes. Their images on display aim to establish an emotional and metaphysical connection with the audience. In the midst of chaos and uncertainty, art can provide a guiding light. We look forward to sharing this experience with you.

參展攝影師

李艾瑪

Emma Lee

鏡像社會

我們所生活的多樣且不斷變化的世界。透過我的鏡頭，我捕捉到日常人們的情感、掙扎和勝利。我利用攝影的力量來提高對重要社會問題的認識，並鼓勵他人在他們的社區中採取積極行動並做出積極的改變。

Reflections of Society

My photographs explore the diverse and ever-changing world we live in. Through my lens, I capture the emotions, struggles, and triumphs of everyday people, showcasing the beauty and complexity of our society. I use the power of photography to bring awareness to important social issues, and to inspire others to take action and make a positive change in their communities.

金大衛

David Kim

木^木之樂章

靈感來自於大自然的美麗和奇妙。我尋求捕捉自然界內存在的和諧與平衡，從日落的鮮豔色彩到樹葉的細緻圖案。通過鏡頭探索大自然，激發他人欣賞和保護我們的環境。

Nature's Symphony

My photography is inspired by the beauty and wonder of nature. I seek to capture the harmony and balance that exists within the natural world, from the vibrant colors of a sunset to the intricate patterns of a leaf. By exploring the wonders of nature through my lens, I hope to inspire others to appreciate and protect our environment.
